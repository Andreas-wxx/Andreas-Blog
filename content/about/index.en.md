## 🙋‍♂️ Who Am I?

Hello! Welcome to my blog. I’m **Andreas**.

I describe myself as an **aviation enthusiast** who is also a "rookie" in the vast world of electronics. I find joy in the rhythmic clacking of a Neovim terminal and the grounded feeling of the smell of solder. Currently, I am navigating the space between binary code and physical circuits, trying to find precise coordinates amidst complex signals.

- 🛰️ **Core Focus**: I am currently diving deep into **GNSS-RTK** high-precision positioning technology and documenting my journey here.
- ⚙️ **Hardware Tinkering**: I spend most of my time with **STM32**, **FPGA (Zynq)**, and various sensors.
- 🎓 **Current Milestone**: Working on my graduation project regarding GNSS-RTK and summarizing my hardware development experience with **Network Monitoring Devices**.
- ⚡ **Fun Fact**:
  - I definitely write bugs faster than I fix them 🐛
  - **The smell of a burnt PCB** is my true nightmare 💥

## 📓 What’s on this Blog?

The minimalist design of the Stack theme is perfect for hosting my technical reflections. Here, I regularly update:
1. **RTK in Practice**: Step-by-step guides from Carrier Phase Differential principles to building your own base station.
2. **Embedded Pitfalls**: Documentation of those annoying MCU bugs that kept me up all night and how I eventually squashed them.
3. **Avionics Musings**: My personal insights and explorations into avionics systems and flight control.
